About Satyajit Ray

Satyajit Ray was a globally renowned Indian filmmaker, writer, and composer known for the humanist realism of his films, which gained Indian cinema international recognition. Ray was deeply involved in every aspect of filmmaking, from scripting and music to cinematography and design.
